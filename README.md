# 🚀 Garment Management System (ASP.NET Core MVC)

A complete **Garment / Shalwar Kameez Production Management System** developed using **ASP.NET Core MVC**.
This system manages the full workflow from **raw material to final delivery**, including expense tracking, production stages, and financial transactions.

---

## 📌 Key Modules

### 💸 Expense Management

* Track daily expenses with auto date & time
* Add expense name and amount
* View complete expense history

---

### 🧵 Fabrics Management

* Import / Export fabric tracking
* Manage supplier records
* Track quantity (KG / Than) and payments
* Outstanding balance calculation

---

### 🎨 Design Module

* MRID & PRINT sections
* Track design production
* Record item quantity (dozen/pieces)
* Payment tracking system

---

### ✂️ Cutting Module

* Assign item numbers
* Track cutting per item
* Record cost and pending balances

---

### 🪡 CMT (Stitching)

* Shalwar, Kameez, Koti modules
* Production tracking per worker
* Payment and pending balance system

---

### 🔘 Kurta Buttons

* Track button work
* Worker-wise assignment
* Payment tracking

---

### 📦 Ending Work

* Cropping
* Pressing
* Packing
* Final stage production tracking

---

### 🧰 Material Management

* Track raw materials (thread, buttons, etc.)
* Expense tracking with details

---

### 📤 Sender (Sales / Delivery)

* Generate item delivery records
* Track items sent to parties
* Maintain billing data

---

### 📥 Receiver (Payments)

* Record incoming payments
* Auto balance update from sender
* Track payment method

---

## ⚙️ Features
